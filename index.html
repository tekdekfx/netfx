Questions
How long does this actually take
Total timeline is two weeks from first call to delivery. Analysis runs 5 to 7 business days. The intake call and delivery walkthrough fit your schedule.
What data do you need from me
Description of each service, rough customer numbers or revenue per offering, and where customers or data overlap between services. I am not auditing your books. Estimates are fine.
What if my services do not have network effects
I will flag which services are independent and which could connect with the right architecture. Sometimes the answer is scale these separately. That is valuable to know before you invest engineering time.
What if my situation is more complex
I will still do the analysis and deliver a roadmap. If complexity requires deeper modeling, financial projections, full technical architecture, or customer research, I will flag that and we can scope it separately.
